<h1 align="left">Hi, I’m Chika.</h1>

<p align="left">
I build continuous-time generative models for image and video, with a focus on multimodal learning and robust generation.
</p>

<p>
I’m a Ph.D. researcher at the University of California, Irvine (
<a href="https://yoonjinwon.github.io/yoonjinwon-site/?pub=selected" target="_blank">Won Lab</a>
), advised by <a href="https://engineering.uci.edu/users/yoonjin-won" target="_blank">Yoonjin Won</a>.
My work sits at the intersection of diffusion/flow-based generative modeling and scientific vision—designing learning signals and training objectives that make continuous-time models more sample-efficient, stable, and robust.
I’ve also collaborated with <a href="https://jd92.wang/" target="_blank">Jindong Wang</a>.
</p>
